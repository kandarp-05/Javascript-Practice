Intro to js
complex js scripts are loaded in another file, the benifit is that the 
browser will download and stores it in cache so that other files too 
can use it. That reduces traffic and makes pages faster.
if src is set, script content will be ignored.
ex:- <script src="script.js"> alert("hello"); <script> // alert will be
ignored.
newline is considered as ";" but sometimes may get error.
for scripts to work in modern way we have to use "use strict" at the 
top in js file.
